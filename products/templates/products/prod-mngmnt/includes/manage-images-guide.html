user guide:

  • Choose 3 images for each piece of art and name them as follows:
    • main image (i.e. image.main)
    • room view image (i.e. image.rv)
    • home page image (i.e. name.hp)
  • Upload the artwork images on AWS S3 bucket.
    • For continuity, upload images from step 1.
    • Once your files are uploaded, find each image url by clicking on it.
  • Add your images to Art.ial
    • For continuity, upload images from step 1.
    • complete the form.
  • Edit your images.
    • You can edit each image by clicking on the name
  • Your images are now available to sort into folders!
